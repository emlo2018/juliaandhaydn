What is the dress code for your wedding

The dress code for our wedding is 'Garden Party'. Think dressy casual, but comfortable

Is there anything that we shouldn't wear?

Tradition in Sweden dictates that the colours red, black or white shouldn't be worn at a wedding.

Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?

The celebrations will be taking place in and around a marquee (weather permitting); we recommend flat-soled shoes or wedges to avoid sinking into the grass. The reception will be held within the marquee with outdoor seating area available on the grass.
